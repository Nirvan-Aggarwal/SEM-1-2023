GOLCONDA FORT

Golconda Fort is a fortified citadel and ruined city located in the western part of Hyderabad, in present-day Telangana, India.The present structure, which was historically known as Qila Muhammadnagar, was built by the Qutb Shahi Sultans of Golconda, during the reign of the first three Sultans, Quli Qutb-ul-Mulk, Jamsheed Quli Qutb Shah, and Subhan Quli Qutb Shah.
